    Iris Zodiac — The Cosmic Currents¶

    Category: Cosmology / Game Mechanics / Lore Foundation Related: All sectors, all species, the Oxir Tribe (HRZ)

    Overview¶

    The Star Atlas galaxy is permeated by a force called Cosmic Currents, emanating from Iris. These currents produce effects on planets and citizens across all zones. The deeper into the zones, the more tangible the effects. The cosmic current influence operates on two dimensions:

    1. Level of Intensity — how strong the manifestation is (Tier 0–5)
    2. Aspect of Iris — what kind of effects are in place (10 aspects)

    Events may trigger cosmic current effects. They can also occur randomly or following pre-established cycles tied to Iris's aspects.

    Intensity Tiers (0–5)¶

    Tier 0 — Ambient Erosion¶

    • No specific aspect exerts influence
    • Cosmic currents act like salt on coastal structures
    • Causes resource durability/spoilage on R20 resources
    • Must be prevented via gameplay loops (warehouses, freighting, storage)

    Tier 1 — Minimal Influence¶

    • Minimal influence of one Iris aspect perceived
    • Minimal buffs and debuffs for activities in the star system
    • Tier 0 effects still in place + minimal buff/debuff to fuel consumption
    • Not strong enough to trigger particular events

    Tier 2 — Small Influence¶

    • Small influence of one Iris aspect perceived
    • Small buffs and debuffs for activities
    • Tier 0 and 1 effects get a slight buff
    • Strong enough to trigger lesser aspect-tied events on the star system

    Tier 3 — Moderate Influence¶

    • Iris aspect clearly perceived
    • Standard buffs and debuffs for activities
    • Tier 0 and 1 effects get a buff
    • Crew members below a certain level get a slight debuff entering this zone
    • Strong enough to trigger aspect-tied events on the star system and lesser events on planets

    Tier 4 — Strong Influence¶

    • Iris aspect strongly perceived
    • Sizable buffs and debuffs
    • Tier 0, 1, and 3 effects are sizably amplified
    • Strong enough to trigger major aspect-tied events on star system and planets
    • Tufa are attracted to Tier 4+ zones

    Tier 5 — CATACLYSM¶

    [!CAUTION] The influence of the Iris aspect reaches its peak, causing distortions in the very fabric of reality.

    • Peak cosmic current manifestation
    • Tier 0, 1, and 3 effects further enhanced
    • Unique aspect-tied events, buffs, and debuffs
    • Huge presence of Tufa
    • Reality itself bends under Iris's power

    The 10 Aspects of Iris¶

    [!NOTE] The comprehension of Iris as aspects comes from the lore of the Oxir Tribe, a civilization in the High-Risk Zone that worships Iris and studies its influence across the galaxy. When the face of Iris changes, the whole galaxy changes with it.

    1. 🌱 Iris Child (Oxian name)¶

    The entire cosmos becomes filled with the energy of renewal.

    • Positive: Limitless potential, renewal, new beginnings
    • Negative: Inquietude, impatience, tenderness
    • The Child brings what is new but requires great care

    2. 🧭 Iris Seeker (Oxian name)¶

    New paths become apparent, motivation overflows, and opportunities appear.

    • Positive: Discovery, motivation, opportunity
    • Negative: Anxiety, imprudence, greed
    • The Seeker opens doors but tempts recklessness

    3. 📚 Iris Priestess (Oxian name)¶

    Knowledge, from the scientific to the mystical, overflows.

    • Positive: Knowledge overflow (scientific and mystical), reflection, study
    • Negative: Physical labor feels tedious, citizens isolate, frustration at high standards
    • The Priestess demands intellectual rigor above all

    4. 🎉 Iris Euphoria (Oxian name)¶

    The whole cosmos feels the call for happiness, the need for connection, and the allurement of desires.

    • Positive: Happiness, connection, satisfaction of desires
    • Negative: Wasteful spending (resources and ATLAS), flustered/depressed if desires unsatisfied
    • Drunk on Euphoria — ecstasy or depression, nothing between

    5. 🐺 Iris Beast (Avatena)¶

    Species show their most raw and visceral forms. Attunement for combat is at its highest.

    • Positive: Peak combat ability, the thrill of the hunt
    • Negative: Disregard for non-combat activities, fear, primordial instincts
    • Exotic beasts and monsters appear across the galaxy
    • Note: Named "Avatena" — not Oxian name

    6. 🛡️ Iris Mother (Oxian name)¶

    Existence is embraced by a feeling of comfort and security, as if a watching god was directing cosmic events.

    • Positive: Safety, comfort, peace, divine protection
    • Negative: Exploration, reward, and adventure diminish
    • The Mother protects but discourages risk

    7. 🌑 Iris Dusk (Vinfid)¶

    An invisible mantle of darkness covers existence, limiting sight and communication in the stars.

    • Positive: Stealth, secrets, infiltration, espionage
    • Negative: Unreliable data/radar systems, lies, silence, crime flourishes
    • "When Iris extends the veil of Dusk, all Punaabs are black" — idiomatic expression meaning identities become indistinguishable under Dusk's darkness (like "at night, all cats are grey")
    • The Order of Season (Pavo Passage) worships this aspect
    • Note: Named "Vinfid" — not Oxian name

    8. 👑 Iris Majesty (Oxian name)¶

    Projects the dual nature of social power to the galaxy.

    • Light side: Benevolent rule, power for the people, nobility in behavior, kindness, justice
    • Dark side: Tyranny, public resources for personal gain, depravity, decay, oppression
    • The Majesty reveals what rulers truly are

    9. 💤 Iris Dreamer (Oxian name)¶

    The Dreamer aspect is a period of rest. The Cosmic Currents' influence diminishes as if Iris was sleeping.

    • Positive: Rest, reduced cosmic interference
    • Negative: Iris sends messages through dreams or nightmares
    • Connects to Mierese dreams of Onato in Glowhaven
    • Iris saves its strength while Dreaming — but still communicates

    10. ⛈️ Iris Tempest (Oxian name)¶

    Tempest is the aspect of destruction. The Cosmic Currents are at the peak of their power.

    • Positive: Oases created in other corners of the galaxy
    • Negative: Powerful galactic storms, destruction wherever they hit
    • "Destruction is the first step for creation"
    • Connects to Everstorm (MRZ-25) — permanently storm-drenched
    • Iris Tempest clears the galaxy for new life

    Naming Conventions¶

    Aspect Name Origin
    1–4, 6, 8–10 Oxian (Oxir Tribe, HRZ)
    5 (Beast) Avatena
    7 (Dusk) Vinfid

    [!IMPORTANT] Two aspects have non-Oxian names — Beast (Avatena) and Dusk (Vinfid). This suggests other civilizations have their own traditions of naming Iris's aspects.

    Gameplay Integration¶

    • Resource spoilage (Tier 0+) drives storage and logistics gameplay
    • Fuel consumption buffs/debuffs (Tier 1+)
    • Aspect-tied events create dynamic, rotating content
    • Mysterious items appear only during certain aspects (e.g., rare flowers)
    • Crew level requirements increase at Tier 3+
    • Tufa presence at Tier 4+ creates ecological/mystical encounters
    • Reality distortion at Tier 5 = endgame-level content
