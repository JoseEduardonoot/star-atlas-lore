No written code â€” a deliberate rejection of everything the Jorvik stand for. Forty years of operations have produced informal realities:

## The Slave Pipeline

The Sorkof run the galaxy's most brazen open pirate slave operation. During raids, crew with valuable skills are taken; the rest released or ransomed. Captives transit via *Gullet* to pre-arranged handoff points, sold to unregulated mining operations, [Pavo Passage](../geography/sectors/pavo_passage.md) labor brokers, and private collectors. Slave sales account for an estimated thirty to forty percent of fleet income.

This practice makes the Sorkof enemies of the Jorvik (ultimate Creed violation), the [Garadar DAC](../factions/garadar_dac.md) (anti-slavery mission), and the [COP](../institutions/council_of_peace.md).
