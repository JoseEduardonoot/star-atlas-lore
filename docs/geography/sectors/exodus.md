# Exodus Sector (MUD Capital)

---

## Overview

> *"It's time humanity frees itself from the shackles of its cradle. Your fate lies elsewhere, deep among the stars. All you need to do is extend your hand and seize your destiny.”*
> — The Revelation.

**Exodus** stands as the glittering testament to God’s promise to humankind. It is the stalwart backbone of the MUD influence within the Galia Expanse, housing the heart and soul of the MUD population and its governing institutions.

## Key Lore

### The Mothership Serene

- **Status**: The monumental vessel that carried humanity from Earth to Galia.
- **Location**: Permanently **docked/stationed** in the Exodus system.
- **Inhabitants**: Serves as the residence for the **MUD Elite**, the Government, and the Holy Family.
- **Symbolism**: The literal and metaphorical heart of the empire.

- **The Philosophy**: Despite being a sanctuary, the name "Exodus" serves as an omnipresent reminder that the journey is **not yet fulfilled**.
- **The Gaze**: MUD eyes are always turned outward, beyond the stars, seeking the true "Paradise" (The Cataclysm).

---

## Political Influence

### Dominant Power: **The Gotti Family** + **The Senate**

- **The Gotti Family** rules from the Mothership Serene. Their word is divine law.
- **The Senate** operates from Exodus as the formal seat of government, but decades of Gotti centralization have left it weakened and largely ceremonial.
- **All factions** maintain embassies and representatives in Exodus, but none hold dominant influence beyond the Holy Family.
- **Tension**: The Senate pleads for restraint and treaties; the Gotti family pursues destiny.
