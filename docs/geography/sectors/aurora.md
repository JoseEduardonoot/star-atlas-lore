# Aurora Sector (MUD 3)

---

## Overview

> *"Always allow yourself to be bathed in the universe's radiant light.”*
> — The Revelation.

**Aurora-II** emerges as a calm spot in the vast cosmic storm. It houses the largest number of MUD citizens beyond Exodus and attracts a variety of species captivated by its **bright white sun**.

## Key Lore

### Calico Industries Hub

- **Status**: Operational hub for several MUD corporations, most notably **Calico** (MUD/ONI joint enterprise).
- **Philosophy**: Strength through diversity. Lighter designs, stealth tech, exotic science.
- **CARY Division**: Dedicated to sustaining life and habitats (agriculture).
- **Vibe**: Serene, star-speckled expanse, busy commerce.

---

## Political Influence

### Dominant Power: **The Synod (The Merchants)**

- Aurora's thriving commerce and diverse population make it the natural seat of Synod power.
- They control contracts, trade routes, and supply chains from the shadows, creating reliance on their networks while others fight over faith and territory.
- **Calico Industries** operates here as the flagship of MUD/ONI integration, further strengthening the Synod's position as the bridge between factions.
- **Tension with Verzan**: The Synod's pragmatic commercialism clashes with the Church's desire for purity and doctrinal control over resources.
