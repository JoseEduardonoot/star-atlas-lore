# Azut Sector (Ustur 4)

> Azut stands as a beacon of progress and harmony.


---

## Overview

**Azut** stands as a beacon of progress and harmony. It is here that **Opal Industries** found its roots and the sprawling **Francor Metropolis** came to life — a shining example of Ustur architectural prowess and their commitment to the Path of Enlightenment.

## Key Lore

### Why Azut, Not Ioki?

- After awakening on Ioki, the Ustur recognized the need for a **new home**.
- Ioki was deemed **too sacred and potentially perilous** for a bustling civilization hub.
- Azut offered a harmonious blend of natural beauty and resource abundance.

### Francor Metropolis

- A **planet-wide city** aglow with billions of **Arco crystals**.
- **Arco Crystals**: Both **functional** (power/processing) and **aesthetic** (visual splendor).
- Cultural, spiritual, and technological center.
- Houses the headquarters of **Opal Industries** (MUD/Ustur collaboration — see [opal.md](../../manufacturers/opal.md)).

### Society

- Azut's inhabitants uphold the **Path of Enlightenment** with unwavering dedication.
- Both technologically advanced and spiritually rich.
- A hub of economic activity **and** a center for philosophical growth.
