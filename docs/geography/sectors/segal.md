## Overview

The chronicles of the **Segal** sector are etched with the valor and heartaches of the Sogmian warriors. For centuries, the Sogmian race found themselves mired in relentless internal conflicts — a consequence of their innate inability to curb their intense impulses.

## Key Lore

### The Sogmian Vows (Du Prah)

- **Origin**: Formulated by the legendary **Segal** (namesake of the sector).
- **Purpose**: A moral compass complemented by the **Noble Houses** system.
- **Result**: Seven illustrious houses, each with distinct ethos, all bound by meritocracy.

### The Scars

- **Vega Fall**: The haunting corruption of **House Akalma**.
- **Convergence War**: Near extinction of the Sogmian people.

### The Present

- From the shadows of their turbulent past, Segal emerges today as a **beacon of genuine peace**.
- Sogmians are **incapable of deception** (the Du Prah forbids it). Their pursuit of peace is sincere.
- Recalibrating perspectives, striving for equilibrium between age-old beliefs and the evolving Galia landscape.

---

## Political Influence

### Dominant Power: **The Noble Houses (Shared Power)**

- The remaining Houses share power through the **expertise of each House**.
- Each House has autonomy in its area of specialization.
- All Houses answer only to the **Sov Aurei** (the supreme Sogmian leader).
