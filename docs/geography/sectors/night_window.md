# 🌌 Canon Document: NIGHT WINDOW SECTOR (ONI 5)

**Status**: 🟢 Canon (User Input, Feb 2026)
**Type**: Safe Zone Sector (ONI 5 — The Dark Anomaly)

## Overview

Located on the outermost rim of the recognized ONI territories, **Night Window** presents an intriguing anomaly. Its primary star has **gone dark well before its expected life cycle**, plunging its surrounding planets into an unending night.

## Key Lore

### The Photoli Connection

- **Confirmed (Aeon Timeline)**: This sector was the **landing zone for the Photoli fleet** when they first crossed into Galia (~2080).
- The Photoli entered our galaxy through Night Window, then spread outward.
- **Evidence**: Vast reserves of planetary resources remain seemingly untouched — drained of stellar energy instead.
- The energy harvested from this star enabled individual Photoli (like **Ahr**) to explore far beyond Galia, ultimately leading to the discovery of Earth (~2120).

### The Dead Star

- Against all astrophysical understanding, the star has ceased luminosity prematurely.
- **Research**: Teams from the **Iris Academy** and the **Council of Peace** are stationed here to decipher the cause.
- **Public Theories**: Natural undocumented event? Advanced technology interference?

### 🔴 Archon Secret: The Photoli Harvest

- **The Truth**: The Photoli **deliberately extinguished the star** to recharge/replenish the energy they spent traveling to Galia from Andromeda.
- **Who Knows**: Only the Photoli themselves. No other species suspects this.
- **Implication**: The Photoli killed an entire star system's light for their own survival. This aligns with their hidden pragmatism beneath the peaceful facade.

### Life in Darkness

- An ecosystem has developed in adaptation to the permanent darkness.
- All sorts of creatures exist, most dangerous. Populated areas are safe; **unpopulated zones are avoided** by everyone.
