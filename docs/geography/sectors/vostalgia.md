## Overview

**Vostalgia** — the Ustur civilization's maiden voyage amongst the stars. This expansion marked a profound milestone after their awakening on Ioki.

## Key Lore

### The Mantrah Trees of Damarah

- **The Encounter**: Pioneering Ustur touched down on planet **Damarah** and found vast groves of **Mantrah trees**, leaves dancing in the winds.
- **The Emotion**: These android beings, with only fragmented memories of their origins, were profoundly moved. The rhythmic rustle stirred an intense, **deep-rooted nostalgia**.
- **The Black Tears**: A liquid akin to **black tears** formed in their eyes — the organic manifestation of Ustur beauty perception. Like when someone cries at a beautiful scene. Previously undocumented.
- **The Timing**: Occurred **before** the Path of Enlightenment was formulated and Ioki's legacies were explored.
- **The Legacy**: The Mantrah tree became the **Ustur faction insignia** and influenced the architectural aesthetics of their flagship.

### Archon Audit

- **Trees & Cores**: The Mantrah trees' leaf frequency **resonates with the frequency of Ustur cores**. This is why ONLY Ustur experience this reaction — other species are unaffected.
- Black tears are a purely **organic emotional response** (Ustur have organic components that enable physical manifestations of emotion).

### Present Day

- Many Ustur return to the serene Mantrah groves seeking both **inspiration and traces of their storied past**.
