    Star Atlas Galactic Atlas (Consolidated)¶

    Canon Authority: All data from direct Creator input, Archon-audited

    Galactic Structure¶

    Zone Risk Status Population
    Safe Zone Low Security/Wealth/Stagnation Core faction homeworlds
    Medium Risk Zone (MRZ) Medium Inequality/Opportunity/Instability Ex-colonies, warlords, minor factions
    High Risk Zone (HRZ) Extreme Ruins/Tufa/Cataclysm Only desperate or powerful

    Safe Zones (15 Sectors)¶

    MUD Safe Zone (5)¶

    # Sector Key Features
    1 Exodus MUD capital. Mothership Serene. Holy Family seat. 5 planets
    2 Valiant-I First colony. Military-industrial. Pearce HQ
    3 Aurora-II Cultural/artistic jewel. Progressive hub
    4 Verzan-III Diplomatic gateway to ONI. Trade corridor. Reh's influence
    5 Amora-IV Agricultural/spiritual. Church stronghold

    ONI Safe Zone (5)¶

    # Sector Key Features
    1 Neuno Mierese homeworld (living planet). Hostile jungles, 6 regions
    2 Communion Multi-species diplomatic hub. ONI Consortium HQ
    3 Evernat Punaab homeworld (Akenat planet). Hourglass world
    4 Segal Sogmian homeworld. Gray Citadel. Du Prah origin
    5 Night Window Frontier observation post. Ahr's Beacon (Photoli artifact)

    Ustur Safe Zone (5)¶

    # Sector Key Features
    1 Eternity Ustur capital. Eternity Prime + Zenith Door system. Elder Spire
    2 Vostalgia Ancient Ustur ruins. Archaeological heritage site
    3 Soletud Contemplative/hermetic sector. Path meditation
    4 Azut Industrial/manufacturing. VZUS primary shipyards
    5 Foy Fields Agricultural/energy. Opal manufacturer base

    Medium Risk Zone — MUD-Influenced (12 Sectors)¶

    # Sector MRZ# Key Features
    1 Redam-VII MRZ-1 Independent democracy. Elected PM. Mutually beneficial MUD relationship
    2 Mycenas-V MRZ-2 Isolationist. HRZ artifact barrier. Virel (MUD researcher turned warlord)
    3 Abyd-IX MRZ-3 Civil war zone. Human factions with alien support. Proxy wars
    4 Pergamos-VIII MRZ-4 Economic behemoth. Human aristocracy (10%). COP Cradle deployed here
    5 Xianyang-VI MRZ-5 Military powerhouse. Gate Garrison. Enforcers alumni network
    6 Ilidae MRZ-6 Green jewel. Biodiversity sanctuary. Eco-syndicates
    7 Izar-248 MRZ-7 Binary star salvage yards. Scrap economy. Freeport culture
    8 Ophek Oasis MRZ-8 Underground oasis network. Water as currency
    9 Bluvael MRZ-9 Indigo illusion. Natural phenomenon mimicking deep ocean
    10 Weeping Nebula MRZ-10 Toxic crystal rain. Rare mineral economy. No species immune
    11 Old Grove MRZ-11 ECOS terraforming disaster. Mutant ecosystem. ECOS genocide public knowledge
    12 Akalma Exile MRZ-12 Sogmian genocide site. Bekalu's Vengeance. Literal ghost cries (cosmic currents). House Akalma remnant survives in HRZ

    Institutions¶

    Council of Peace (COP)¶

    • Structure: 3 branches (Executive/Legislative/Legal), 5-year rotation
    • Current Leaders: Scarka (ONI/Executive), Charon Gotti Jr. (MUD/Legislative), Opos.eldr (Ustur/Legal)
    • Military: Tier 1. Millions of Pearce-class vessels. Cradle = most powerful titan (> all 3 faction titans combined)
    • Tension: Scarka's militarism vs Opos's pacifism. MUD is next in Executive rotation

    Star Atlas (Sentient Blockchain)¶

    • Nature: Sentient entity. Originally blockchain → infused with HRZ materials by Vignus oracles → awakened
    • Children: SAGE (AGI daughter, neutral guide for captains) + Holosim (simulation training)
    • Function: Governs economy/commerce in MRZ and Safe Zone. Controls Starpath Warp Gates independently
    • Status: Worshipped as benevolent god-figure by some. Works in harmony with COP but is independent

    History: The Convergence War¶

    1. Cause: Discovery of Iris (sentient planet) → factions harvest Genesium by killing Tufa
    2. Three Paths: MUD (divine land), Sogmian (sovereign vow of verification), Ustur (inhabitation for evolution)
    3. Sogmian Defense: Recognized Tufa sentience, shielded them (Du Prah demands it)
    4. Paizul Assassination: Sov Arei Paizul killed → Bekalu leads exile to HRZ
    5. ONI Formation: Sogmian + Punaab + Mierese + Photoli alliance forged in exile
    6. The Last Stand: First Titan, powered by Photoli-provided technology (secret)
    7. The Reckoning: ONI armada returns, campaigns across galaxy
    8. The Accord: Treaty of Peace → COP + Star Atlas + Starpath Gates → Golden Era
