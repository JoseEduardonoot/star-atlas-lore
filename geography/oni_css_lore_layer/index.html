    C4 Lore Layer — The Neuno Region¶

    "We were born in a prison. Our god is a prisoner. And somehow we run the galaxy. If that's not funny, nothing is." — Mierese joke, attributed to everyone

    The Neuno Region (R-ONI-CSS) is the beating heart of the ONI Consortium — a seven-system Safe Zone anchored by the sentient living planet Neuno and the rotating Central Space Station that serves as ONI's galactic headquarters. Here, four species govern as one: Mierese storytellers, Sogmian warriors, Punaab merchants, and Photoli ancients share the most culturally diverse sovereign space in the galaxy. The region's character is defined not by a single species' authority but by their collective refusal to die alone — a principle forged in HRZ exile and inscribed in the Maharog chronicle: "Gilded by Punaab gold, hardened by Sogmian discipline, guided by Photoli wisdom, driven by Mierese prowess."

    The Architecture of the Neuno Region¶

    "ONI is a choir of four voices singing in four languages. Beautiful — until you realize each voice has its own song." — High Prelate Cassandra Vael, Church of MUD

    The Neuno Region is unlike any other capital space in the galaxy. Where MUD's Exodus Region radiates outward from a single human holy site, ONI's capital is a constellation of cooperation — each system reflecting a different facet of the four-species alliance.

    At the center orbits Neuno itself — a 3,000-year-old space parasite fused with a planet's geological structure, whose immune system attacks its own inhabitants with tsunamis, droughts, and poisoned crops. The Mierese love it anyway. Surrounding this living world, the Central Space Station rotates its architectural style with each presiding species — currently displaying Mierese design under Volmik Vaor Scarka's leadership.

    The CORE systems house the institutional backbone of the Consortium: cultural preservation, diplomatic infrastructure, and the administrative apparatus that keeps four species arguing productively. The SEC systems provide the economic and military foundation — tritium refineries, Punaab trade posts, Sogmian patrol routes, and classified Hikibashi installations that officially do not exist.

    The region's resource profile is dominated by Tritium Ore — the fuel that powers warp travel and military fleets. Combined with substantial Aluminum, Hafnium, and Copper deposits, the Neuno Region is self-sufficient in the strategic minerals needed to sustain both the ONI fleet and the COP Cradle that Vaor Scarka commands.

    👑 The Neuno System (CSS-ONI-KING-01)¶

    "Neuno is not a home. Neuno is a relationship. A complicated, abusive, beautiful, irreplaceable relationship." — Mierese saying

    Overview¶

    The KING system of R-ONI-CSS is the gravitational and political center of the entire ONI Consortium. It contains the sentient planet Neuno — homeworld of the Mierese — and the Central Space Station, ONI's rotating headquarters. Every major decision affecting four species and seventeen territories passes through this system.

    Neuno's radiant blue sun feeds the living planet through photosynthesis, driving cycles of lush growth and months-long hibernation. When Neuno sleeps, its verdant jungles become desolates of dust, its enormous animals shelter underground, and the pace of civilization slows to match the planet's breathing. When it wakes, the terrain morphs rapidly, reflecting the fluctuating temperaments of the sentient homeland — and the Mierese draw resilience from this ever-shifting landscape.

    The 7 Worlds of the Neuno System¶

    🌍 Neuno (P1) — The Living Home¶

    "The planet seems alive because it IS alive — a colossal space parasite using the crust and mantle as makeshift organs." — Iris Academy xenobiology survey, classified
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P1
    Type Type-3
    Resources 56+ ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Copper Ore, Garnet Crystals
    Population ~480 million (primarily Mierese, with multi-species diplomatic quarters)
    Controlling Authority Mierese of Neuno — under the elected Speaker
    Key Installations Resonance Halls (Lore Keeper acoustic chambers), Morvalel Marsh settlements, Eskscarka Currents coast (Hikibashi birthplace), Kaljura Singing Sands, Grand Tale Assembly site

    Neuno is a singularity — a sentient entity the size of a planet, born when a colossal space parasite collided with the world in the galaxy's primordial era and "infected" its geological structure. Growth originates from the southern pole, and the planet's biological immune system treats the Mierese as surface parasites, punishing expansion with natural disasters.

    The planet is divided into six ancestral regions, each giving its Mierese inhabitants their third name:

    Region Third Name Character
    Singing Sands of Kaljura Jura Desert dunes that produce harmonic tones — the most poetic Mierese
    Vireko Ravines Eko Deep geological fissures with extreme biodiversity — the boldest explorers
    Aerqint Spires Qint Towering rock formations piercing the atmosphere — the most isolated communities
    Morvalel Marsh Valel Vast wetlands teeming with life — traditional heartland of the Lore Keepers
    Umbyara Woods Yara Dense, hostile jungles full of deadly predators — the toughest warriors
    Eskscarka Currents Scarka Treacherous ocean currents — birthplace of the Hikibashi and Vaor Scarka

    The Resonance Halls — deep-ocean acoustic chambers where the geometry of stone and water creates extraordinary properties for vocal recitation — are the cultural heart of Mierese civilization. Here, Chief Lore Keeper Ondara Valel guards the Grand Tale's 47 Chapters, including the three Sealed Chapters whose contents are known only to her and two successors.

    Neuno communicates through subtle sensations rather than words — only the wisest Lore Keepers attempt to interpret these feelings. When the Benevolent Explorers arrived in ~2377, they terraformed portions of the planet, advanced Mierese science, and left without explanation. Their identity remains one of the galaxy's great unsolved mysteries.

    🌍 Vaorkesh Station (P2) — The Central Space Station¶

    "No single species owns the Consortium. The station proves it — every rotation, a new face in the mirror." — ONI diplomatic protocol briefing
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P2
    Type Barren (Orbital station complex)
    Resources 15 ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Copper Ore, Iron Ore, Garnet Crystals
    Population ~12 million (all four ONI species + diplomatic residents)
    Controlling Authority ONI Consortium — presiding species rotational governance
    Key Installations Central Space Station (ONI HQ), Consortium Council Chambers, COP Liaison Office, Species Delegation Quarters, Punaab Trade Exchange

    The Central Space Station is the headquarters of the ONI Consortium — a massive orbital installation whose architectural style rotates to reflect the currently presiding species. Under Volmik Vaor Scarka's Mierese leadership, the station displays flowing organic forms, bioluminescent lighting, and acoustic corridors designed for oral storytelling rather than written display. When the Sogmians next preside, the same corridors will be refaced in gray stone and Du Prah inscriptions.

    The CSS houses the Consortium Council Chambers — the forum where all four species delegations negotiate policy. Major decisions require consensus across all four member civilizations, making ONI simultaneously the most representative and the most argumentative faction in the galaxy.

    The station also serves as the primary COP liaison point for the ONI Safe Zone and as a commercial exchange managed by Punaab merchants. Diplomatic delegations from MUD, the Ustur Regency, and neutral institutions maintain permanent quarters — making the CSS the most species-diverse single installation in sovereign space.

    🌍 Morvalim (P3) — The Keeper's Reach¶

    "The Grand Tale functions like a blockchain — distributed across Lore Keepers' memories, incorruptible because no single Keeper holds the whole." — Ondara Valel, on the oral archive's resilience
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P3
    Type Type-3
    Resources 28 ⭐⭐ — Hafnium Ore, Tritium Ore, Garnet Crystals, Copper Ore, Quartz Crystals
    Population ~35 million (Mierese majority, Sogmian garrison)
    Controlling Authority Mierese Lore Keepers (cultural stewardship)
    Key Installations Grand Tale Recitation Hall, Lore Keeper Training Academy, Annual Assembly Site, Sogmian Honor Guard barracks

    Morvalim serves as the off-world extension of the Lore Keepers' cultural infrastructure — a secondary archive world where young Keepers train in the art of memorization and recitation. Unlike Neuno's Resonance Halls, which are sacred and restricted, Morvalim's facilities are accessible to ONI citizens of all species who wish to hear public chapters of the Grand Tale.

    The planet houses the secondary Annual Assembly Site — the location where elder Lore Keepers reunite each Neuno year to share collected stories and vote on which should be incorporated into the Grand Tale. The primary site remains on Neuno, in an unknown location that even the Hikibashi has not confirmed.

    A Sogmian Honor Guard maintains a permanent barracks on Morvalim, providing security for the cultural infrastructure at the personal request of the Kur Faists — a gesture that embodies the Du Prah principle: "provide shelter for those who seek it." The Lore Keepers tolerate the guards. They do not request them.

    🌍 Oreli (P4) — The Still Depths¶

    "The Drowning has a fifteen percent graduation rate. The ocean does not negotiate." — Hikibashi recruitment summary, classified
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P4
    Type Type-5
    Resources 17 ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Copper Ore, Hafnium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Iron Ore
    Population ~800,000 (classified — true population unknown)
    Controlling Authority Hikibashi (classified)
    Key Installations Classified. Officially: "Mierese Oceanic Research Station"

    Oreli is, on official maps, a Mierese oceanic research station — an unremarkable Type-5 world with subsurface water reserves and modest mineral deposits. Its population records list approximately 800,000 marine researchers and their support staff.

    In reality, Oreli is one of the Hikibashi's primary training installations. Its deep ocean zones simulate the treacherous Eskscarka Currents of Neuno — the birthplace of the Three Currents doctrine. Recruits undergo The Drowning here: the Hikibashi's selection program, conducted in extreme marine environments where the graduation rate is fifteen percent.

    The Hikibashi's existence is officially denied at every level of Mierese government. Oreli's true purpose is known only to the Commandant, Vaor Scarka, and the forty to sixty operatives who survived its ocean floors.

    🌍 Virekal (P5) — The Ravine Gardens¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P5
    Type Type-0
    Resources 17 ⭐⭐ — Arco, Tritium Ore, Hafnium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Nitrogen
    Population ~22 million (Mierese, Punaab)
    Controlling Authority Mierese civilian government + Punaab Agricultural Consortium
    Key Installations Ravine Terrace Farms, Punaab Cold-Chain Processing, Arevok Grazing Reserves

    Named for the Vireko Ravines of Neuno, Virekal is the primary agricultural world of the Neuno system. Its deep geological fissures create microclimates that support intensive farming — Mierese-adapted crops in the upper terraces, Punaab cold-chain produce in the lower chambers.

    Arevok herds — grazing animals whose meat is prized across Mierese settlements — roam managed reserves on the surface plateaus. The Punaab Agricultural Consortium manages the cold-chain logistics that distribute food across the system, a partnership that embodies ONI's cooperative model: Mierese grow what the land provides, Punaab sell it to everyone else.

    🌍 Kalju Point (P6) — The Singing Outpost¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P6
    Type Type-3
    Resources 15 ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Copper Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Iron Ore
    Population ~8 million (Mierese dominant)
    Controlling Authority Mierese civilian government
    Key Installations Narrative Battle Amphitheaters, Kaljura cultural exchange, Community Lore Circle

    Named for the Singing Sands of Kaljura, Kalju Point is the entertainment and cultural capital of the Neuno system — a world where Mierese art forms flourish. Its most famous institutions are the Narrative Battle Amphitheaters, where bards face off in the Mierese equivalent of rap battles: oral competitions where reputation, wit, and the ability to weave a story in real time determine social standing.

    The planet is also home to one of the most active Community Lore Circles — informal gatherings where ordinary Mierese share their personal stories in hope of being selected by a regional Lore Keeper for incorporation into larger narratives. Being featured in a bigger story is the supreme honor in Mierese civilization; Kalju Point is where that ambition begins.

    🌍 Umbelkor (P7) — The Dark Canopy¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-KING-01-P7
    Type Type-2
    Resources 22 ⭐⭐ — Garnet Crystals, Tritium Ore, Copper Ore, Iron Ore, Hafnium Ore
    Population ~5 million (Mierese majority)
    Controlling Authority Mierese civilian government
    Key Installations Umbyara Survival Schools, Hunting Lodge Network, Wildlife Conservation Zone

    Named for the Umbyara Woods of Neuno — the dense, hostile jungles that produce the toughest Mierese warriors — Umbelkor is a Type-2 world whose thick canopy ecosystems serve as wilderness training grounds. Young Mierese from across the system come here to learn survival skills in conditions that mirror Neuno's most dangerous regions.

    The planet's Garnet Crystal deposits are harvested by small-scale mining operations, but Umbelkor's primary value is cultural rather than economic: it preserves the wilderness ethic that defined Mierese survival on Neuno before the Benevolent Explorers arrived. The Hikibashi recruits candidates who excel in Umbelkor's survival circuits — though this connection is never acknowledged.

    🔵 The Dosk System (CSS-ONI-CORE-01) — The Consortium's Engine¶

    "We are four species who learned to speak with one voice. That does not mean we agree. It means we have learned that speaking alone gets you killed." — ONI delegation briefing, the Maharog tradition

    Overview¶

    The Dosk System is the institutional core of the ONI Consortium — where the administrative, diplomatic, and inter-species governance infrastructure operates. Named in tribute to the Mierese tradition of multi-layered cooperation, this CORE system houses the permanent delegations of all four ONI species and the bureaucratic apparatus that keeps the Consortium functional.

    The 4 Worlds of the Dosk System¶

    🌍 Doskel (P1) — The Four Quarters¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-01-P1
    Type Type-3
    Resources 45+ ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Copper Ore, Iron Ore
    Population ~95 million (all four ONI species)
    Controlling Authority ONI Consortium joint governance
    Key Installations Species Delegation Headquarters (×4), Multi-Species Commerce Hub, Ogrika Satellite Administration, Consortium Data Archive

    Doskel is the administrative capital planet of the ONI Consortium — distinct from the Central Space Station, which handles executive decision-making. While the CSS hosts the Council Chambers and the presiding leader's office, Doskel manages the day-to-day governance of seventeen territories across three galactic zones.

    The planet is organized into Four Quarters — each architecturally reflecting one of ONI's member species. The Sogmian Quarter features austere gray stone and Du Prah training yards. The Punaab Quarter glitters with trade halls and Ogrika shipyard administration offices. The Mierese Quarter flows with organic corridors and acoustic spaces designed for oral communication. The Photoli Quarter is the smallest and the quietest — luminous chambers where the ancient beings conduct their counsel through light rather than sound.

    🌍 Selkara (P2) — The Apprentice's Vigil¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-01-P2
    Type Type-5
    Resources 22 ⭐⭐ — Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Tritium Ore, Copper Ore, Iron Ore
    Population ~18 million (Mierese, Sogmian)
    Controlling Authority Mierese educational authority
    Key Installations Grand Tale Youth Academy, Multi-Species Language Institute, Mierese Diplomatic Training

    Selkara is named for the youngest Lore Keeper apprentice in modern history — Selkora Scarka, chosen at age six as a potential successor to Chief Lore Keeper Ondara Valel. The planet serves as the educational hub of the Neuno system, focused on training the next generation of Mierese for inter-species interaction.

    The Multi-Species Language Institute is a rarely discussed but crucial institution: Mierese have no written language and consider written communication "severely lacking, almost useless." The Institute trains young Mierese to work with written systems used by humans, Ustur, and Sogmians — a pragmatic compromise that the Grand Tale records as "learning to speak in someone else's silence."

    🌍 Qintara (P3) — The Spire Watch¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-01-P3
    Type Barren
    Resources 15 ⭐ — Aluminum Ore, Iron Ore, Hafnium Ore, Tritium Ore, Copper Ore
    Population ~3 million (multi-species military)
    Controlling Authority ONI Combined Defense Command
    Key Installations Neuno System Defense Array, Sogmian Patrol Coordination, Fleet Drydock Auxiliary

    Named for the Aerqint Spires of Neuno — the most isolated Mierese communities — Qintara is the primary defense installation of the Neuno system. A barren world repurposed for military infrastructure, it houses the Sogmian-commanded defense array that protects the ONI capital.

    The Sogmian patrol coordination center manages fleet deployments across the entire Safe Zone — six Noble Houses providing warriors trained from childhood in the Du Prah tradition. Qintara's location within the CORE system reflects ONI's philosophy: defense is not peripheral; it is fundamental.

    🌍 Busan Anchorage (P4) — The Builder's Berth¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-01-P4
    Type Type-3
    Resources 22 ⭐⭐ — Iron Ore, Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Manganese Ore
    Population ~14 million (Sogmian, Punaab)
    Controlling Authority House Busan (Sogmian) + Ogrika Harbors (Punaab)
    Key Installations Ogrika Satellite Yards, House Busan Engineering Division, Fleet Maintenance Drydocks

    Named for the Sogmian Noble House that built The Last Stand mk. VIII — the first Titan-class warship — Busan Anchorage is the ship manufacturing and maintenance hub of the Neuno system. House Busan's engineering division operates alongside Ogrika Harbors satellite yards, producing and maintaining the vessels that keep ONI's fleet operational.

    The partnership between House Busan (Sogmian shipbuilders) and Ogrika (Punaab manufacturers) is one of ONI's oldest and most productive cross-species collaborations. Busan provides engineering excellence; Ogrika provides manufacturing scale and commercial distribution. Together, they built the fleet that keeps ONI a Tier 1 military power.

    🔵 The Maharog System (CSS-ONI-CORE-02) — The Nexus of Commerce¶

    "Gilded by Punaab gold, hardened by Sogmian discipline, guided by Photoli wisdom, driven by Mierese prowess." — The Maharog chronicle, describing the ONI Consortium's founding

    Overview¶

    Named for the Maharog — the founding chronicle that recorded the alliance of four species in HRZ exile — this CORE system is the economic and diplomatic engine of the Neuno Region. Punaab trade networks, Photoli counsel chambers, and the diplomatic infrastructure that maintains ONI's galactic relationships all operate from here.

    The 3 Worlds of the Maharog System¶

    🌍 Maharokel (P1) — The Golden Dock¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-02-P1
    Type Type-3
    Resources 60+ ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Copper Ore, Iron Ore
    Population ~72 million (Punaab dominant, all four species present)
    Controlling Authority Punaab Trade Council + ONI Commerce Bureau
    Key Installations Punaab Grand Exchange, Diplomatic Quarter (MUD, Ustur, COP), Calico Regional Office, Merchant Prince Liaison

    Maharokel is the commercial capital of the Neuno Region — the world where Punaab trade networks intersect with galactic diplomacy. The Punaab Grand Exchange handles the trade volume that sustains ONI's Tier 1 economic status, connecting the Safe Zone's resource production to galactic markets through established Punaab merchant routes.

    The Diplomatic Quarter hosts permanent missions from MUD, the Ustur Regency, and the Council of Peace — as well as a Calico regional office. Calico, the MUD/ONI diplomacy-focused ship manufacturer, maintains its ONI presence here, producing vessels with stealth and exotic science capabilities.

    A discrete Merchant Prince Liaison office connects the Neuno Region to the Denebula Utopia financial enclave — where ONI representatives Yacob (Sogmian, House Exinade) and Mevertrase (Mierese) sit among the galaxy's six wealthiest individuals.

    🌍 Ohravel (P2) — The Photoli Anchor¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-02-P2
    Type Type-0
    Resources 15 ⭐ — Tritium Ore, Hafnium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Iron Ore, Copper Ore
    Population ~900,000 (Photoli, with multi-species researchers)
    Controlling Authority The Photoli — Traveler delegation
    Key Installations Photoli Counsel Chambers, Luminary Exchange, Iris Academy Exchange Campus

    Ohravel is the Photoli's permanent presence in the Neuno Region — a Type-0 world whose minimal atmospheric interference allows unobstructed observation of stellar and cosmic phenomena. The Photoli — beings older than every other civilization in the galaxy — maintain their counsel chambers here, though their motives for joining a consortium of younger species remain only partially explained.

    The Luminary Exchange is where Photoli Travelers share knowledge with ONI scholars under carefully controlled conditions. The Photoli gave House Garveil the technology for the Titan's power source during the Convergence War; their generosity continues in measured doses. What they receive in return — access to Mierese oral histories, Sogmian military data, Punaab trade intelligence — is officially "knowledge exchange." Whether this is the complete picture remains one of ONI's unanswered questions.

    An Iris Academy Exchange Campus operates nearby, hosting researchers who study the Photoli's cosmic knowledge under the Academy's neutral mandate.

    🌍 Pathra's Rest (P3) — The White Lantern¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-CORE-02-P3
    Type Type-1
    Resources 15 ⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Copper Ore, Iron Ore, Hafnium Ore
    Population ~6 million (multi-species)
    Controlling Authority ONI humanitarian affairs + Sons of Patrah
    Key Installations Sons of Patrah Sanctuary, Galia Medical Union Campus, ONI Humanitarian Coordination

    Named in honor of Patrah — the Sogmian of House Outro who founded the galaxy's foremost humanitarian order alongside the Ustur surgeon Eolus.tcher — Pathra's Rest serves as the medical and humanitarian hub of the Neuno Region.

    The Sons of Patrah maintain a permanent sanctuary here, operating under the white lantern — the only symbol in the galaxy that halts all hostilities. The Galia Medical Union campus provides species-neutral healthcare for all four ONI species and the diplomatic community.

    ONI's humanitarian coordination office manages aid operations across the MRZ from this world — including the ongoing reconstruction of Frenir, liberated by Vaor Scarka's COP incursion in ~2624.

    🟢 The Eskri System (CSS-ONI-SEC-01) — The Current's Edge¶

    Overview¶

    The SEC-01 system is the primary industrial zone of the Neuno Region — where the strategic minerals that sustain ONI's fleet and economy are extracted and processed. Named for the Eskscarka Currents (shortened to Eskri in common usage), the system's identity reflects the Mierese region that produced both the Hikibashi and Vaor Scarka himself.

    The 3 Worlds of the Eskri System¶

    🌍 Azkorra (P1) — The Tritium Well¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-01-P1
    Type Type-3
    Resources 43 ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Iron Ore, Copper Ore
    Population ~28 million (multi-species)
    Controlling Authority ONI Mining Consortium (Punaab-managed, multi-species labor)
    Key Installations Tritium Deep-Core Extraction, Punaab Refinery Complex, Worker Settlement Grid

    Azkorra is the warp fuel capital of the Neuno Region — the primary source of the Tritium Ore that powers ONI's fleet, the COP Cradle, and galactic Starpath travel. Deep-core extraction operations run year-round under Punaab management, processing the Type-3 world's substantial mineral deposits into refined fuel stock.

    The Worker Settlement Grid houses multi-species labor crews — Mierese extraction teams (their fine motor control excels at precision mining), Sogmian heavy-equipment operators, and Punaab logistics coordinators. A Mierese saying captures the arrangement: "The Mierese find it, the Sogmians carry it, the Punaab sell it, the Photoli explain why."

    🌍 Zurkai (P2) — The Forge Below¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-01-P2
    Type Type-2
    Resources 22 ⭐ — Iron Ore, Copper Ore, Manganese Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore
    Population ~11 million (Sogmian, Mierese)
    Controlling Authority House Busan Industrial Division
    Key Installations Zurkai Smelting Complex, Raw Material Processing, Strategic Reserve Stockpile

    Zurkai handles the heavy processing that turns extracted minerals into construction-grade alloys. House Busan's Industrial Division manages the smelting operations with Sogmian efficiency — every kilogram of processed material tracked through Du Prah inventory disciplines that predate spaceflight.

    The Strategic Reserve Stockpile maintains a wartime buffer of refined materials — a lesson from the Convergence War, when the scattered ONI species nearly starved before Punaab merchants organized the supply chains that kept 10,000 Sogmian refugees alive. The stockpile exists because ONI remembers what happens when supply lines fail.

    🌍 Merkova (P3) — The Sifter's Camp¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-01-P3
    Type Barren
    Resources 15 ⭐ — Hafnium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Iron Ore, Copper Ore, Tritium Ore
    Population ~2 million (contracted labor, automated extraction)
    Controlling Authority Punaab Mining Syndicate
    Key Installations Automated Extraction Grid, Ore Staging Platform, Transport Hub

    A barren world stripped to efficient purpose — Merkova is an automated extraction site managed by a Punaab Mining Syndicate. Its population is largely transient: contracted workers rotating through six-month shifts, operating alongside automated systems that extract ore for transport to Zurkai's smelting complex.

    🟢 The Akenat System (CSS-ONI-SEC-02) — The Merchant's Reach¶

    Overview¶

    Named in tribute to the Punaab homeworld of Akenat, SEC-02 is the trade and commerce hub of the Neuno Region — the system where goods flow between the ONI Safe Zone and the broader galactic economy.

    The 3 Worlds of the Akenat System¶

    🌍 Sivaalen (P1) — The Exchange¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-02-P1
    Type Type-3

    Population ~42 million (Punaab dominant, all species)
    Controlling Authority Punaab Trade Council
    Key Installations Punaab Free Market, Cargo Transit Hub, Ogrika Distribution Center, Currency Exchange

    Sivaalen is the trade gateway of the Neuno Region — the planet through which most commercial traffic enters ONI's capital space. The Punaab Free Market operates here under Trade Council governance, managing the flow of goods between the Safe Zone and the MRZ trade corridors.

    The Ogrika Distribution Center extends the Punaab shipbuilder's reach beyond manufacturing — handling distribution logistics for Ogrika-built vessels across the ONI sphere of influence. A saying among Punaab merchants: "A Punaab will sell you your own ship and make you think you got a bargain."

    🌍 Duumari (P2) — The Deep Guild¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-02-P2
    Type Type-5

    Population ~9 million (Punaab Profound majority)

    Key Installations Profound Guild Halls, Artisan Workshops, Subsurface Manufacturing

    Named for Duumakkerund, King of the Guilds — the Profound Punaab monarch who rules from below the Sea of Mist on Akenat — Duumari is the Profound Punaab settlement in the Neuno Region. Where Sivaalen reflects High Punaab commercial flamboyance, Duumari embodies Profound Punaab pragmatism.

    The planet's subsurface manufacturing facilities produce the specialized components that feed Ogrika's ship production — circuit boards, navigation modules, life-support systems. Profound Punaab guild discipline ensures quality standards that exceed every competitor's specifications.

    Key Installations High Court Diplomatic Salon, Punaab Cultural Exhibition, Barrot Entertainment Franchise

    Named for Rikkaatovaalu, the High Punaab King of the Merchant Princes, Rikvaal is the social and diplomatic hub of the Punaab community in the Neuno Region. Where Sivaalen handles trade and Duumari handles manufacturing, Rikvaal handles relationships — the High Punaab art of making every species feel like they're getting the better deal.

    A Barrot Entertainment franchise operates here, providing the galaxy's premiere entertainment experiences to ONI's diplomatic community. The Punaab Cultural Exhibition showcases the duality of the Punaab civilization — High flamboyance above, Profound pragmatism below — and invites visitors to experience both.

    🟢 The Ohravim System (CSS-ONI-SEC-03) — The Watcher's Orbit¶

    Overview¶

    SEC-03 is the research and observation system of the Neuno Region — where Photoli cosmic knowledge, Iris Academy expertise, and ONI's scientific infrastructure converge.

    The 3 Worlds of the Ohravim System¶

    🌍 Ohravim Prime (P1) — The Lens¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-03-P1
    Type Type-3
    Resources 43 ⭐⭐ — Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore, Iron Ore, Copper Ore
    Population ~19 million (multi-species academic)
    Controlling Authority ONI Science Bureau + Iris Academy joint governance
    Key Installations Iris Academy Regional Campus, Photoli Astronomical Observatory, Xenobiology Institute, Scriptorium Liaison Archive

    Ohravim Prime is the scientific capital of the Neuno Region — the intersection of Photoli cosmic observation, Iris Academy research, and Mierese xenobiology. The Iris Academy Regional Campus operates under the Academy's neutral mandate, hosting researchers from all factions.

    The Photoli Astronomical Observatory monitors stellar phenomena across the ONI Safe Zone — and, unofficially, maintains observation protocols that extend toward the Cataclysm. The Photoli's Dawnbreak fortress faces inward toward Iris; the Observatory's instruments face outward. Whether these are complementary or competing observation systems remains one of ONI's unanswered questions.

    A Scriptorium of the Lumikir Liaison Archive maintains a permanent presence — the Lumikir information brokers exchange written records with the Lore Keepers' oral tradition, creating the galaxy's most significant example of complementary knowledge systems. Ondara Valel considers the Scriptorium's archivists "the closest thing the Mierese oral tradition has to kindred spirits in another culture."

    🌍 Talorim (P2) — The Quiet Lab¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-03-P2
    Type Type-3
    Resources 22 ⭐ — Tritium Ore, Garnet Crystals, Hafnium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Copper Ore
    Population ~7 million (multi-species research staff)
    Controlling Authority ONI Science Bureau
    Key Installations Neuno Planetary Studies Center, Parasite Biology Lab, Agricultural Research

    Talorim exists to answer the question every species asks about ONI's homeworld: how does a planet live? The Neuno Planetary Studies Center coordinates scientific research into the sentient planet's biology — its parasitic infection of the geological structure, its immune responses, its communication through subtle sensations.

    The Parasite Biology Lab studies Neuno's organism from a safe distance, analyzing samples, monitoring seismic data that correlates with the planet's "moods," and attempting to forecast the cycles of growth and hibernation that define life on the surface.

    🌍 Galaskir (P3) — The Far Signal¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-03-P3

    A barren world repurposed as the communications backbone of the ONI Safe Zone. Galaskir's Deep Space Relay Array connects all ONI territories — from the five Safe Zone sectors to the eleven MRZ influence zones — through encrypted channels that the Hikibashi Undertow monitors for intrusion.

    🟢 The Segalir System (CSS-ONI-SEC-04) — The Gray Shield¶

    Overview¶

    Named for Segal the Wise — the founder of the Du Prah who transformed feral Sogmian combatants into a civilization — SEC-04 is the military frontier of the Neuno Region. Sogmian patrols, fleet staging areas, and classified Hikibashi counterintelligence operations defend the approaches to the ONI capital.

    The 3 Worlds of the Segalir System¶

    🌍 Segalir Station (P1) — The Shield Wall¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-04-P1
    Type Type-3
    Resources 43 ⭐⭐ — Iron Ore, Copper Ore, Tritium Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore
    Population ~25 million (Sogmian military, multi-species support)
    Controlling Authority Sogmian Combined Fleet Command
    Key Installations Fleet Staging Area, Sogmian Warrior Academies, Du Prah Training Grounds, House Command Halls (×6)

    Segalir Station is the military heart of the Neuno Region — where six Noble Houses maintain their command cadres and the Sogmian Combined Fleet coordinates ONI's conventional defense. Du Prah training grounds run continuous warrior preparation programs: Sogmian soldiers are trained from childhood, and their oath-bound vows make them terrifyingly committed. They will not retreat, will not break oaths, and will die before dishonor.

    Each of the six Noble Houses — Busan (builders), Xictus (diplomats), Outro (humanitarians), Garveil (technologists), Exinade (financiers), and Akalma (banished, representation vacant) — maintains a command hall here. The vacant Akalma hall is maintained in perfect condition: a monument to the house Bekalu devastated, and a reminder that Prince Jefos may one day return from HRZ exile to claim it.

    🌍 Paizulen (P2) — The Memorial¶
    C4 ID CSS-ONI-SEC-04-P2
    Type Type-5
    Resources 15 ⭐ — Tritium Ore, Iron Ore, Copper Ore, Aluminum Ore, Hafnium Ore
    Population ~4 million (Sogmian, multi-species memorial)
    Controlling Authority Sogmian Kur Faist Council
    Key Installations Convergence War Memorial, Paizul's Flame (eternal vigil), Exile Remembrance Archive

    Named for Sov Arei Paizul — Bekalu's wife, whose assassination on 2520-07-17 triggered the exile that created ONI — Paizulen is a world of remembrance and reckoning. The Convergence War Memorial lists every Sogmian who died in the near-extinction, when the species was reduced from millions to approximately 10,000 survivors.

    Paizul's Flame burns eternal — a vigil maintained by rotating Sogmian honor guards from every Noble House. The memorial is not sentimental; it is functional. Every Sogmian recruit visits Paizulen before completing their Du Prah training, to understand what the oaths they swear were written to prevent.

    Controlling Authority Classified
    Key Installations Classified. Officially: "Deep Space Monitoring Station"

    Officially a deep-space monitoring station operated by Sogmian intelligence. In practice, Xictara houses the Hikibashi Undertow's counterintelligence coordination center — the nerve center from which Mirsk Eko monitors threats to the ONI capital, tracks potential infiltrators, and maintains the surveillance protocols that protect the Neuno Region from external espionage.

    The planet also serves as the coordination point for ONI's intelligence assessments of galactic threats — from Jorvik pirate movements to Dark Photoli activity monitoring conducted jointly with the Photoli Sih Rihim.
