# 🌌 Canon Document: VERZAN SECTOR (MUD 4)

**Status**: 🟢 Canon (User Input, Feb 2026)
**Type**: Safe Zone Sector (MUD 4)

## Overview

> *"Trust in our paths crossing again. When they do, humankind shall witness the realization of my promise."*
> — The Revelation.

**Verzan** is a sanctified nexus where **God Ahr** was last heard from before his departure.

## Key Lore

- **Environment**: Unforgiving landscapes and harsh living conditions.
- **Population**: Devout pilgrims and enterprising settlers who have built sprawling metropolises.
- **Conflict**: Recent murmurs of discord between religious factions and the broader populace.

### The Order of Light

- **Nature**: Ancient congregation of Photoli worshippers. Created by **Photoli Reh** to gain influence.
- **Location**: Principal temple on a moon devoid of resources (purchased from MUD).
- **Secret**: Reh shares minor powers with seniors to **brainwash** recruits.
- **Archon Inference**: The Order predates Verzan, but Reh **expanded to Verzan deliberately** — Ahr's departure left a spiritual vacuum that made this population especially susceptible to Photoli-worship.

---

## Political Influence

### Dominant Power: **The Holy Servants (The Church)**

- Verzan is the spiritual capital of MUD. The Church rules here with an iron hand, controlling pilgrimages, tithes, and doctrinal interpretation.
- **Internal Rivalry**: The **Order of Light** (Photoli Reh's creation) competes for spiritual allegiance. The Church views them as heretical, but their growing influence among the populace is undeniable.
- **Tension with Valiant**: Church orthodoxy clashes with Scientia's rationalism. The eternal war of faith vs reason plays out between these two sectors.
