## Overview

**Neuno** — "Living Home" in the Mierese tongue — is the enigmatic heart of the Mierese lineage. The sentient planet is so renowned that the entire system bears its name.

## Key Lore

### The Sentient Planet

- **Dual Role**: Nurturing guardian **and** inescapable warden of the Mierese.
- **Communication**: Neuno communicates like an animal — **subtle sensations** (not words). The wisest **Lore Keepers** attempt to interpret these feelings.
- **Not Iris**: Unlike Iris (sentient, fractured mind, cosmic currents), Neuno is more primal/instinctual.
- **Resources**: Rich in **biomass and food**.

### The Liberation

- **Seclusion Broken**: An **unknown extraterrestrial species** arrived and guided the Mierese into the cosmic age.
- **Mystery**: Their identity remains unexplored. (Future lore hook.)

### The Living Terrain

- Terrains morph rapidly, reflecting the **fluctuating temperaments** of the sentient homeland.
- The Mierese draw resilience from this ever-shifting landscape.

### Mierese Rise

- From planetary prisoners to helming the **ONI Consortium** in a mere few centuries.
- Their bond with Neuno remains unbroken.
