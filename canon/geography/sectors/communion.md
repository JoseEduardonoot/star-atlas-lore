## Overview

- **~2498**: **Communion** is formed as the **first multi-species colony sector** in Galia, composed of **Punaabs, Miereses, Photoli, and Sogmians** — setting the seeds for the formation of the **ONI Faction**

Once marred by relentless battles as the ONI races vied for dominance, **Communion** has transformed from a war-torn prize into a beacon of unity. It is a sanctuary for those weary of age-old rivalries and binding traditions.

## Key Lore

### The Melting Pot

- **Refugees of Dogma**: Many Punaabs and Sogmians seeking to escape the rigid shackles of their cultural dogmas have found solace here.
- **Individualism**: A place where individualities are celebrated rather than suppressed by clan or house.
- **Archon Inference (Punaab)**: Communion is becoming a **Third Way** for Punaabs — neither High nor Profound, but "Free Punaabs." This radical individualism may threaten the Dual Monarchy long-term, creating a tripartite conflict: High (capitalism) vs Profound (guilds) vs Communion (pure individualism).

### The Wild Side

- **Biodiversity**: A kaleidoscope of exotic wildlife. Rite of passage for fledgling hunters.
- **Nimrod Trackers**: A DAC of **monster hunters** (minor faction). Hunt exotic fauna only. (Details TBD.)

### Governance

- **Rotating Leadership**: A **multispecies council** with representatives of all species.
- **Status**: Well-accepted and functional. No single species dominates.
- **~2550**: Communion becomes the **commercial capital of the ONI Consortium** — the most prosperous and interconnected ONI sector
- **Investment**: Vast ATLAS investment to elevate Communion into an intergalactic marvel destination.

## Political Influence

### Dominant Power: **Multispecies Rotating Council**

- Unlike other sectors dominated by a single species, Communion is governed collectively.
- High Punaab investment is significant but governance is shared.
