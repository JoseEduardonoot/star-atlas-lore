# Council of Peace (COP)

> The Council of Peace (COP) is the cornerstone of interstellar governance in the Galia Expanse.


---

## Overview

The **Council of Peace (COP)** is the cornerstone of interstellar governance in the Galia Expanse. Born from the ashes of the Convergence War to uphold the fragile balance of power among MUD, ONI, and Ustur. Both a symbol of hope and a battleground of ideologies, where diplomacy and ambition constantly collide.

---

## Origin

- **~2523**: Created during **The Accord** (post-Convergence War settlement)
- Born alongside the **Star Atlas** blockchain and **Starpath Warp-Gates**
- Emerged from the **Treaty of Peace** signed after ONI's Reckoning campaign — all 3 faction leaders sign the Treaty, establishing open sharing of all scanned data of space, including fleet movements and installations
- Implementation led to the **Golden Era** (current canonical timeframe)

---

## Government Structure: Three Branches

Leadership rotates every **galactic cycle (~5 Old Earth years)**. Each faction appoints their leader as representative. No single faction gains undue influence.

| Branch | Current Leader | Faction | Role |
|--------|---------------|---------|------|
| **Executive** | **Volmik Vaor Scarka** | ONI (Mierese) | Ruling Secretary. Military incursions into MRZ (Frenir, Pergamos). Controversial. **MUD is next in rotation.** |
| **Legislative** | **Charon Gotti Jr.** | MUD (Human, female) | Political stability & cooperation. Diplomatic counterbalance to Scarka's militarism. Under scrutiny for her silence on controversies. |
| **Legal** | **Opos.eldr** | Ustur | Bastion of resistance against militarism. Enacted rulings demanding cessation of MRZ hostilities. Craftsman turned leader. |

### Internal Tensions

- **Scarka vs Opos**: Militarism vs pacifism. Scarka frames MRZ campaigns as anti-piracy; Opos demands cessation.
- **Charon's Silence**: Growing unrest within MUD about her non-intervention on Scarka's policies.
- **Ideological Divide**: Expansionism vs non-interference principles.
- **Pattern**: Conflicts between branches are recurring, but **Scarka is actively inflaming** existing tensions beyond the norm.

## Military Power

### Galactic Force Index: **Tier 1** (Unmatched)

- Millions of **Pearce-class vessels**, countless advanced warships
- Billions of soldiers drawn from all three factions
- Even the **combined** fleets of MUD, ONI, and Ustur would falter against COP's scale

### Mothership Cradle (The Titan)

- **~2526**: Construction begins on the Cradle — the most powerful vessel ever built.
- **~2617**: Construction of the Cradle is **completed** — the most powerful vessel in galactic history is operational.
- Equipped with unmatched technological advancements.
- Capable of overwhelming even the most formidable opponents.
- **Recently deployed**: Against Pergamos and Frenir in the MRZ — sent shockwaves across the galaxy.

---

## Economic Power

### Galactic Wealth Index: **Tier 1** (Wealthiest Institution)

- **Starpath Warp Gates** are operated by the **Star Atlas** (sentient blockchain) independently, but Star Atlas works in harmony with COP and submits to its legal jurisdiction.
- Regulatory authority over galactic trade ensures stability.
- Funds large-scale reconstruction, military, and bureaucratic apparatus through taxation and trade regulation.

---

## Political Power

### Galactic Political Index: **Tier 1** (Ultimate Authority)

- Primary governing body in Galia Expanse.
- Rulings dictate trade flow, territorial disputes, and foundational laws (**Star Atlas Accords**).
- Tri-faction leadership ensures no single faction dominates.
- Collective authority supersedes even the most powerful individual factions.

## Post-War Infrastructure

| Institution | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **Council of Peace** | Galactic governance and peacekeeping |
| **Star Atlas** | Economic infrastructure (sentient blockchain) |
| **Starpath Warp-Gates** | Physical infrastructure connecting sectors across the void. **~2526**: Construction begins. **~2596**: Construction ends — ushering in **prosperity across the Safe Zone** |

### ECOS Membership

- **~2527**: The **ECOS** (independent environmental/ecological faction) attempts to gain **major faction status** within the COP. The **MUD lobby** successfully blocks this, relegating ECOS to a **member without voting rights**.

### The Current Age

- **~2620**: The **Current Age** of Star Atlas begins — the present canonical timeframe in which the galaxy's political tensions, faction rivalries, and unresolved mysteries drive the narrative forward.
- **~2621**: **Vaor Scarka** becomes **Ruling Secretary** of the Council of Peace (ONI's turn in rotation)
- **~2623**: The **Council of Peace intervenes in the Medium Zone** — COP forces attack **Frenir**, **Pergamos**, and the **Jorvik Fleet** in a decisive military campaign
- **~2623**: The **Events of Never Alone** begin — a major canonical narrative arc
- **~2623**: Under pressure from MRZ factions (led by **Redam**) and internal faction leadership, the COP **accepts a truce** in the Medium Zone, limiting its military activities
