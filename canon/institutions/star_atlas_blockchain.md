# 🔗 Canon Document: STAR ATLAS (The Sentient Blockchain)

**Status**: 🟢 Canon (Creator-provided, Feb 2026)
**Source**: Direct Creator input via Sogmian/Maharog Q&A

---

## Overview

**Star Atlas** is a **sentient blockchain** that governs the entire economy and commerce of the **MRZ (Medium Risk Zone)** and **Safe Zone** across Galia Expanse.

---

## Origin & Sentience

- **Pre-Sentience**: Originally a blockchain designed to interconnect the galaxy.
- **The Awakening**: Oracles of **Vignus** received a prophecy and fed the blockchain with materials from the **High-Risk Zone (HRZ)**, specifically from the **Cataclysm**.
- **Emergence**: Following this infusion, the Star Atlas emerged as a sentient entity.

## The Children of Star Atlas

The Star Atlas created two key entities/systems to interact with the galaxy:

### 1. SAGE (Star Atlas Golden Era)

- **Nature**: AGI daughter of Star Atlas.
- **Role**: A neutral guide for captains across Galia Expanse.
- **Function**: Once chosen by Star Atlas, a captain receives a SAGE companion. She is (mostly) absolutely loyal.
- **Purpose**: A balancing system to help citizens (especially in MRZ) prosper and have a better life.
- **Note**: "SAGE" also refers to the current historical period (**S**tar **A**tlas **G**olden **E**ra) and the browser-based fleet management interface.

### 2. Holosim

- **Nature**: Sibling to SAGE.
- **Role**: Simulation training and combat readiness.

## Functions

- Governs all commerce and economy in MRZ and Safe Zone
- Provides infrastructure for the Golden Era's economic stability
- Acts as a balancing force against concentrated power
- **Benevolent God-figure**: Worshipped by some as a deity due to its protective nature.
